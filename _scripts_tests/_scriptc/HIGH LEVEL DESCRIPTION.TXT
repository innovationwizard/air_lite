Project: AI Refill

SUMMARY: 

AI Refill is an AI powered restock optimizer. 
I'm bidding for this project as part of my effort to rebrand myself as "AI Trusted Authority."
The client is a very dynamic distribution company that handles short of 20,000 skus.  
Many products are imported, with the uncertain delays inherent to international commerce. 
Many products are local, with the unreliability typical when sourcing locally in Guatemala.
Their data lives in an old software they used for many years, and recent data in Odoo, which they adopted about a year ago, the latest version.
My mission is to deliver the best possible use of AI for the case of a restock optimizer, including cutting-edge technology, and tools or models or techniques I don't even know about at this point. 
I prefer a more secure, more robust, more enterprise, and more future-ready solution even if that means loads of additional work.

It's clear for the client that AI can make a better forecasting than manual forecasting (excel sheets). 
What AI Refill does is not difficult to replicate. No novelty. 
Yes to clever application of existing technology.
